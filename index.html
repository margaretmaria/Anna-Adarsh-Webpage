Sundaresan Rajasekaran is a System Administrator Intern of Computer Science at George Washington University. He is Ph.D. candidate of Computer Science Department at the George Washington University, Washington, DC, under the advisement of Dr. Tim Wood. He received M.S. degree at the George Washington university in 2010 and Bachelor of Engineering (B.E.) in 2008 from Anna University, Chennai, India. His research focuses on Cloud Computing, Virtualization, Performance and Security in Distributed Systems. Sundaresan Rajasekaran has been awarded as Entrepreneur Lead, I-Corps by National Science Foundation at Stanford University for Commercialization Feasibility Research and Demonstration Preparation for Privacy-Preserving Location Based Services. His alma mater is the George Washington University in Washington, DC. Sundaresan Rajasekaran is also referred to as Sunny Rajesekaran.
